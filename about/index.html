Education and work

I am a BA in East Asian studies, focusing on Korea and Japan. Currently I am finishing my MA on language technology in the University of Helsinki. Since my interests lie in languages in general, my minor subjects — linguistics, language technology and sociology were chosen to support the study of East Asian languages.

In addition to Korean, Japanese and basic Chinese, I also have studied several European languages — Portuguese, Spanish, French and Italian in addition to English, Swedish and my mother tongue Finnish.

I have also studied information engineering and media technology on university level but dropped these after getting an opportunity to study at a prestigious Finnish university.
